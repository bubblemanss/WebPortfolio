TurfWarz


Tech Stack: Node.js, Azure, Android, Microsoft Band
Adding spice to your daily jog, you can capture turf by starting from a single point, running your route and returning to the starting point. The boundary and all the area within is captured and becomes yours, but others can take it if they overlap with your territory after you capture it. Furthermore the app will stop tracking your route if your heartbeat and pace is too low, which is detected using the Microsoft Band, for more than 5 seconds meaning you are not being active enough and therefore do not deserve the area. If you want to gain more turf and keep your existing turf, you have to work for it!

Won best use of Microsoft at BostonHacks

AnswerMeThis


Tech Stack: Node.js, Wolfram API, Twilio

A Twilio app that when texted a mathematical question or a single word dictionary query, it will reply with the correct answer and relevant images. This is useful for people with no internet and still want to connect to the web for homework help. Third world countries generally have access to mobile phones but may not have data or wifi, therefore this can be extremely helpful there.

Won best use of Wolfram at HackWestern
